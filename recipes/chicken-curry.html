Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Curry Recipe for Beginners. This detailed post on how to make chicken curry at home will guide you to make the best dish using pantry staples. This is one of the easiest that even a novice or a bachelor can cook with an amazing outcome. It not only tastes delicious but is also very flavorful with soft, tender & succulent pieces of chicken. Serve it with Butter naan tandoori roti, Jeera rice or plain basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatos
  • Ginger TGarlic paste
  • Onions
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Yougurt + Lime Juice
  • Ghee + Oil
  • Coconut milk

Steps

Preparation

  1. First heat oil in a deep pan, then add whatever whole spices you have â€” bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves & green cardamoms. Fry them till you get an aroma, just for a min.
  2. Add chopped onions and chilies. Saute till the onions turn deep golden in color.
  3. Next add ginger garlic paste and saute till you get a nice aroma. This just takes about a minute.
  4. Then add tomatoes, turmeric and salt. Mix well and cook until the tomatoes turn soft & mushy.

  5. Pour yogurt (or cashew paste), red chili powder, garam masala & coriander powder.

    On a low heat, cook until the mixture smells good.

  6. Add chicken and coriander leaves. Fry till the chicken turns white or pale in color. This takes around 3 mins.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 3 or 4 mins. The step is very important as chicken absorbs all the flavours of the spices
  8. Meanwhile in a seperate pot, heat up 1 cup water. Using cold wates most times toughens the chicken so it is good to use hot water.

How to make chicken curry

  1. Pour hot water just enough to make a thick curry. Do not add to much water. Chicken curry will become runny. Sometimes chicken lets out lot of moisture. So use only as needed.
  2. Cover and cook on medium to low flame till the chicken is softly cooked and the courry turns thick.
  3. Taste the curry & add more salt and masala if needed.
  4. Cover and cook on a low flame for 3 to 5 mins. When the chicken is cooked completely it will fall of the bone easily.
  5. Lastly garnish with coriander leaves, cover and off the heat. Serve chicken curry with Naan or jeera rice.
